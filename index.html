About

"Welcome to NUTAN SHIKSHA NIKETAN, a CBSE-affiliated school from Balvatika 1 (NUR) to 10+2. Our school is the best CBSE school in this locality, offering a comprehensive education that fosters academic excellence, moral values, and all-round development. At NUTAN SHIKSHA NIKETAN, we aim to nurture students into worthy citizens of our country, equipped with habits of study, discipline, self-reliance, and moral values. Our school is an English medium institution, co-educational and open to students of all backgrounds. Owned and managed by the Swami Vivekanand Trust, we strive to provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment that encourages students to grow and thrive. We invite all of you to explore our campus and discover the NUTAN SHIKSHA NIKETAN difference."
